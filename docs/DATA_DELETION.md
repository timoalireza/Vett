# Data Deletion Policy

**Last Updated:** December 1, 2024  
**Version:** 1.0

## 1. Introduction

This Data Deletion Policy explains your rights to request deletion of your personal data from Vett, how we process deletion requests, and what data may be retained for legal or operational reasons.

This policy supplements our [Privacy Policy](/privacy) and is designed to comply with data protection laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for users in the European Union.

## 2. Your Right to Deletion

Under GDPR Article 17 (Right to Erasure) and other applicable data protection laws, you have the right to request deletion of your personal data in certain circumstances, including:

- The data is no longer necessary for the original purpose
- You withdraw consent for data processing
- You object to processing and there are no overriding legitimate grounds
- The data has been unlawfully processed
- The data must be deleted to comply with a legal obligation

## 3. How to Request Data Deletion

### 3.1 Account Deletion (Self-Service)
The easiest way to delete your data is to delete your account:

1. **Mobile App:**
   - Open the Vett app
   - Go to Settings
   - Select "Delete Account"
   - Confirm deletion

2. **Contact Support:**
   - Email: support@vett.xyz
   - Subject: "Account Deletion Request"
   - Include your account email or user ID

### 3.2 Partial Data Deletion
If you want to delete specific data without deleting your entire account:

- **Delete Individual Analyses:** Use the delete function in the app for each analysis
- **Unlink Social Accounts:** Go to Settings > Linked Accounts and unlink Instagram or other accounts
- **Contact Support:** Email support@vett.xyz with specific deletion requests

### 3.3 Instagram DM Bot Data Deletion
To delete Instagram-related data:

1. **Unlink Instagram Account:**
   - Open the Vett app
   - Go to Settings > Linked Accounts
   - Select "Unlink Instagram Account"

2. **Delete Instagram User Data:**
   - Unlinking your account will delete your Instagram user ID association
   - DM messages are not permanently stored (only analysis results are stored)
   - Contact support if you need additional Instagram data deleted

## 4. What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account or request data deletion, we will delete the following:

### 4.1 Account Information
- **User Account:** Your user record, email, display name, avatar URL
- **Authentication Data:** External ID associations (Clerk/Firebase)
- **Profile Information:** All profile data associated with your account

### 4.2 Analysis Data
- **All Analyses:** All analyses you created, including:
  - Submitted content
  - Analysis results (scores, verdicts, summaries)
  - Extracted claims
  - Sources and references
  - Explanation steps
  - Attachments (images, documents, links)
- **Analysis Metadata:** Timestamps, status, input types

### 4.3 Instagram DM Bot Data
- **Instagram User ID:** Your Instagram account identifier
- **Instagram Username:** If stored separately
- **Account Linking Data:** Verification codes, linking timestamps
- **Usage Records:** Instagram DM analysis usage tracking
- **Note:** DM messages themselves are not permanently stored; only analysis results derived from them

### 4.4 Social Account Linking Data
- **Platform User IDs:** Instagram and other social platform identifiers
- **Verification Codes:** Temporary codes used for account linking
- **Linking Records:** Timestamps and status of account linking

### 4.5 Subscription Data
- **Subscription Records:** Your subscription plan, billing cycle, status
- **RevenueCat Data:** Customer ID associations (deleted from our systems)
- **Note:** Payment records may be retained by RevenueCat and payment processors per their policies

### 4.6 Usage and Analytics Data
- **API Request Logs:** GraphQL queries and mutations associated with your account
- **Feature Usage:** Records of which features you used
- **Error Logs:** Technical errors associated with your account

### 4.7 Feedback Data
- **Feedback Records:** All feedback you provided on analyses
- **Agreement Status:** Your agreement/disagreement with analysis results
- **Comments:** Any comments you submitted

### 4.8 Collections
- **All Collections:** Any collections you created
- **Collection Items:** All items within your collections

## 5. What May Be Retained

Certain data may be retained for legal, operational, or security reasons, even after account deletion:

### 5.1 Legal Obligations
We may retain data if required by law, including:
- **Tax Records:** Financial transaction records (if applicable)
- **Legal Holds:** Data subject to legal proceedings or investigations
- **Regulatory Compliance:** Data required by regulatory authorities
- **Court Orders:** Data subject to court orders or subpoenas

### 5.2 Anonymized Data
We may retain anonymized, aggregated data that cannot identify you:
- **Aggregated Analytics:** Usage statistics and trends
- **Research Data:** Anonymized data used for service improvement
- **Performance Metrics:** System performance and reliability data

### 5.3 Security and Fraud Prevention
We may retain limited data for security purposes:
- **Security Logs:** Records of security incidents or suspicious activity
- **Fraud Prevention:** Data necessary to prevent fraud or abuse
- **Account Recovery:** Limited data for account recovery verification (if applicable)

### 5.4 Backup Systems
Data may persist in backup systems for a limited time:
- **Backup Retention:** Backups are typically retained for 30-90 days
- **Automated Deletion:** Backup data is automatically deleted after the retention period
- **No Active Access:** Backup data is not actively accessible or used

### 5.5 Third-Party Services
Some data may be retained by third-party services:
- **Payment Processors:** RevenueCat and payment providers retain transaction records per their policies
- **Authentication Providers:** Clerk may retain authentication data per their policies
- **Cloud Providers:** Infrastructure providers may retain logs per their policies

## 6. Deletion Timeline

### 6.1 Immediate Deletion
The following are deleted immediately upon account deletion:
- Account access and authentication
- Active subscription (cancelled immediately)
- Ability to create new analyses

### 6.2 Deletion Processing
Most data is deleted within **30 days** of your deletion request:
- Account information
- Analysis data
- Social account linking data
- Usage records
- Feedback data

### 6.3 Backup Cleanup
Backup data is automatically deleted within **90 days**:
- Automated backup systems purge data after retention period
- No manual intervention required

### 6.4 Legal Holds
If data is subject to a legal hold:
- Deletion is suspended until the hold is lifted
- You will be notified if a legal hold affects your deletion request
- Data will be deleted promptly after the hold is released

## 7. Instagram DM Bot Specific Deletion

### 7.1 Unlinking Instagram Account
When you unlink your Instagram account:
- **Immediate:** Your Instagram user ID is disassociated from your Vett account
- **Within 30 days:** Instagram usage records are deleted
- **Note:** DM messages sent to @vettapp are not stored by us; only analysis results are stored

### 7.2 Deleting Instagram Analysis Results
Instagram DM analysis results are stored as regular analyses:
- Delete them individually through the app, or
- Delete your entire account to remove all Instagram-related analyses

### 7.3 Meta/Instagram Data
- We do not control data stored by Meta/Instagram
- DM messages remain in your Instagram account per Meta's policies
- To delete Instagram DMs, use Instagram's deletion features

## 8. Verification of Deletion

After processing your deletion request, we will:

1. **Confirm Deletion:** Send a confirmation email (if email address is still available)
2. **Provide Timeline:** Inform you of when deletion will be complete
3. **Answer Questions:** Respond to any questions about retained data

## 9. Data Portability Before Deletion

Before deleting your account, you may request a copy of your data:

- **Request Format:** Email support@vett.xyz with subject "Data Export Request"
- **Data Format:** We will provide data in a structured, machine-readable format (JSON)
- **Timeline:** Data export requests are processed within 30 days
- **Scope:** Includes all personal data we hold about you

See our [Privacy Policy](/privacy) for more details on data portability rights.

## 10. Children's Data

If you are a parent or guardian and believe we have collected data from a child under 13:

- **Immediate Deletion:** We will delete the data immediately upon verification
- **Contact:** Email support@vett.xyz with subject "Child Data Deletion Request"
- **Verification:** We may request verification of parental/guardian status

## 11. Third-Party Data Deletion

We cannot delete data held by third-party services directly. You may need to:

- **RevenueCat:** Contact RevenueCat to delete subscription data
- **Clerk:** Contact Clerk to delete authentication data
- **Meta/Instagram:** Use Instagram's deletion features for DM data
- **Payment Processors:** Contact payment processors for transaction data

We will assist you in identifying which third-party services hold your data.

## 12. Exceptions and Limitations

### 12.1 Legal Requirements
We cannot delete data if:
- Required by law or court order
- Necessary for legal claims or defense
- Required for regulatory compliance
- Subject to legal hold or investigation

### 12.2 Anonymized Data
We may retain anonymized data that cannot identify you:
- Aggregated statistics
- Research data
- Performance metrics

### 12.3 Public Content
If you have shared content publicly:
- Publicly shared analyses may remain accessible until manually deleted
- Collections shared with others may remain accessible
- We will make reasonable efforts to remove public content upon request

## 13. Reinstatement After Deletion

After account deletion:
- **Permanent:** Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone
- **New Account:** You may create a new account, but previous data cannot be recovered
- **No Recovery:** Deleted data cannot be recovered or restored
