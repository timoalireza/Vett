## 3. Information We Collect

### 3.1 Account Information
When you create an account, we collect:
- **External ID:** Unique identifier from authentication provider (Clerk/Firebase)
- **Email Address:** Your email address (if provided)
- **Display Name:** Your chosen display name (if provided)
- **Avatar URL:** Profile picture URL (if provided)
- **Account Creation Date:** Timestamp of account creation

### 3.2 Analysis Data
When you submit content for analysis, we collect:
- **Submitted Content:** Text, links, images, videos, or audio you submit
- **Analysis Results:** Scores, verdicts, confidence levels, summaries, recommendations
- **Extracted Claims:** Factual statements identified in your content
- **Sources:** References and fact-checking sources used in analysis
- **Explanation Steps:** Reasoning chains generated by AI models
- **Metadata:** Analysis timestamps, status, input types

### 3.3 Instagram DM Bot Data
When you use our Instagram DM Bot, we collect:
- **Instagram User ID:** Your Instagram account identifier (from Meta API)
- **Instagram Username:** Your Instagram username (if available)
- **DM Messages:** Text messages, links, and image attachments you send to @vettapp
- **Verification Codes:** Codes you send to link your Instagram account
- **Usage Tracking:** Number of analyses requested via Instagram DM, usage periods
- **Account Linking Status:** Whether your Instagram account is linked to your Vett account

### 3.4 Social Account Linking Data
When you link social accounts (e.g., Instagram), we collect:
- **Platform User ID:** Your identifier on the social platform
- **Verification Codes:** Temporary codes used for account linking
- **Linking Timestamps:** When accounts were linked or unlinked
- **Platform Type:** The social platform (currently Instagram)

### 3.5 Subscription and Payment Data
When you subscribe to a paid plan, we collect (via RevenueCat):
- **Subscription Plan:** FREE, PLUS, or PRO tier
- **Billing Cycle:** Monthly or annual
- **Subscription Status:** Active, cancelled, past due, or trialing
- **RevenueCat Customer ID:** Identifier from RevenueCat
- **Subscription IDs:** Transaction identifiers from RevenueCat

**Note:** We do not directly process payment information. Payment processing is handled by RevenueCat and their payment providers.

### 3.6 Usage and Analytics Data
We collect information about how you use the Service:
- **API Requests:** GraphQL queries and mutations
- **Analysis Submissions:** Number and frequency of analyses
- **Feature Usage:** Which features you use and how often
- **Error Logs:** Technical errors and performance issues
- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system (for mobile app)

### 3.7 Feedback Data
When you provide feedback on analyses, we collect:
- **Agreement Status:** Whether you agree or disagree with analysis results
- **Comments:** Any comments you provide
- **Feedback Timestamps:** When feedback was submitted

### 6.1 Data Collection

### 6.2 Data Processing

- **Account Linking:** Verification codes are used to link your Instagram account to your Vett account
- **Result Delivery:** Analysis results are sent back to you via Instagram DM

### 6.3 Data Storage
- **Instagram User IDs:** Stored in our database to track usage and link accounts
- **DM Messages:** Processed but not permanently stored (only analysis results are stored)

### 6.4 Meta/Instagram API
- We use Meta's Instagram Graph API to send and receive messages
- Your use of the Bot is subject to Meta's Privacy Policy and Instagram's Data Policy
- We comply with Meta's API terms and data usage requirements

### 6.5 Opt-Out
You may opt out of Instagram DM Bot services by:
- Unlinking your Instagram account through the Vett app
- Ceasing to send messages to @vettapp
- Deleting your Vett account (see Section 9)
